---
title: "📞 Import from Twilio"
description: "To enable calling via Twilio, follow this two-step setup process to connect your Twilio number with ConverseKit"
---

### 🧩 Step 1: Import Twilio Number in ConverseKit Web App

1. Log in to the [**ConverseKit Web App**](#).
2. Navigate to the **Phone Numbers** section.
3. Click **“Import from Twilio”**.
4. Fill in the required details:
   - `Twilio Phone Number` (e.g., \+1XXXXXXXXXX)
   - `Name` – A friendly label for your internal reference (e.g., “Sales Line”)
   - `Twilio Account SID`
   - `Twilio Auth Token`
5. Click **Import** to complete the process.

> ✅ Once imported, your number will appear in the list with a unique **External ID**.\
> This ID will be used to connect the number within your Salesforce org.

---

### 🧩 Step 2: Register Number in ConverseKit Salesforce App

1. Open the **ConverseKit app** inside Salesforce.
2. Navigate to the **Phone Numbers** tab.
3. Click **“Import”**.
4. Enter the `External ID` obtained in Step 1.
5. Select the `Assistant` you want to associate with this number.
6. Click **Import** to complete the setup.

> 📞 Your Twilio number is now fully connected to ConverseKit and ready to handle inbound and outbound calls through your Assistant.
